## General Information | معلومات عامة

**Q: What is Campus51?**
A: Campus51 is an online education platform that offers professional certifications, diplomas, and career-development courses designed to help learners upgrade their skills and advance their careers.

**Q: What types of programs does Campus51 offer?**
A: Campus51 offers:
- Professional certificates
- Diplomas
- Blended training programs
- Instructor-led courses and workshops
- On-demand self-paced courses

**Q: Are the courses online or offline?**
A: Most Campus51 programs are delivered online (synchronous and asynchronous), allowing students to learn flexibly from anywhere.

**Q: Who can enroll in Campus51 courses?**
A: Anyone interested in developing professional skills, changing careers, or improving qualifications can enroll, depending on program requirements.

## Accreditation & Certification | الاعتماد والشهادات

**Q: Are Campus51 certificates accredited?**
A: Yes. Campus51 is an accredited training provider by the **CPD Standards Office (UK)**. All programs undergo rigorous assessment and validation to ensure quality, credibility, and alignment with international standards. Upon successful completion, candidates receive globally recognized CPD hours and certification.

**Q: Who are the CPD Standards Office?**
A: The CPD Standards Office is an internationally recognized accreditation body based in the United Kingdom that evaluates and accredits professional training providers and educational programs. Their accreditation ensures learning activities meet high-quality standards for Continuing Professional Development (CPD).

**Q: Are any Campus51 courses approved by KHDA UAE?**
A: Yes. Selected Campus51 courses and training programs are approved by the **KHDA (Knowledge and Human Development Authority)** in Dubai, UAE. KHDA approval reflects compliance with recognized educational and training quality standards within the UAE. KHDA-approved programs provide added value for professionals seeking recognized training credentials in the UAE and GCC region. Please contact support to confirm whether a specific program is KHDA approved.

**Q: Will I receive a certificate after completing the course?**
A: Yes. Students who successfully complete course requirements receive a certificate of completion with globally recognized CPD hours.

**Q: Are the certificates digital or printed?**
A: Most certificates are issued digitally. Some programs may offer printed certificates upon request.

**Q: Can I add my certificate to LinkedIn?**
A: Yes, digital certificates can be added to LinkedIn and professional profiles.
