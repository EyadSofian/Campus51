# Category 08 — Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) Programs | برامج تحليل السلوك التطبيقي

**Audience:** SEN teachers, shadow teachers, ABA practitioners, early intervention specialists, parents of children with developmental needs.
**Focus:** ABA principles, behavior intervention, early detection, and supporting children with autism and developmental delays.

---

## Course: General ABA – Level 1 & 2 (RBT / IBT / QBT) | تحليل السلوك التطبيقي – المستوى 1 و 2

**EN Description:** Comprehensive overview of behavioral principles and interventions, preparing practitioners for international certifications: RBT (Registered Behavior Technician), IBT (International Behavior Technician), and QBT (Qualified Behavior Technician).

**AR Description:** فهم شامل لمبادئ التدخلات السلوكية، تحضيرًا للحصول على شهادات RBT/IBT/QBT الدولية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Master ABA principles and ethics.
- Apply behavior intervention strategies in clinical and educational settings.
- Prepare for international ABA certification exams.

**Recommended For:** Shadow teachers, ABA aides, early-career behavior analysts.

---

## Course: Introduction to Early Detection of Developmental Delays | مقدمة في الكشف المبكر عن التأخر النمائي

**EN Description:** Recognize developmental milestones and early indicators of delay, and learn how to refer appropriately for assessment and intervention.

**AR Description:** التعرف على مراحل النمو والمؤشرات المبكرة للتأخر وكيفية الإحالة المناسبة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Identify red flags across developmental domains.
- Apply screening tools used in early intervention.
- Make appropriate, timely referrals.

**Recommended For:** Early years teachers, KG staff, nursery educators, school nurses.

---

## Course: Foundations of Applied Behavior Analysis | أساسيات تحليل السلوك التطبيقي

**EN Description:** Understand the foundational principles of behavior analysis—including reinforcement, prompting, fading, shaping, and chaining.

**AR Description:** فهم المبادئ الأساسية لتحليل السلوك التطبيقي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Define and apply the 7 dimensions of ABA.
- Use reinforcement schedules effectively.
- Build behavior intervention plans.

**Recommended For:** New ABA practitioners, SEN teachers, shadow teachers.

---

## Course: Reinforcement & Pairing | التعزيز والإقران

**EN Description:** Apply reinforcement effectively to encourage positive behaviors and build a positive relationship between the practitioner and the learner.

**AR Description:** بناء تفاعل إيجابي مع الطلاب لزيادة المشاركة والانضباط.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Master pairing as the foundation of behavior work.
- Identify reinforcers (primary, secondary, social, tangible).
- Use reinforcement to build positive behavior trajectories.

**Recommended For:** ABA aides, parents, special education teachers.

---

## Course: Functions of Behavior | وظائف السلوك

**EN Description:** Identify behavioral functions (attention, escape, access, sensory) and develop preventive strategies.

**AR Description:** تحديد أسباب السلوك ووضع استراتيجيات وقائية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Conduct functional behavior assessments (FBA).
- Match interventions to behavior function.
- Replace challenging behaviors with functional communication.

**Recommended For:** ABA practitioners, school counselors, classroom teachers facing challenging behaviors.

---

## Course: Strategies for Gaining Student Cooperation | استراتيجيات كسب تعاون الطلاب

**EN Description:** Build engagement and compliance through positive relationships and antecedent-based strategies.

**AR Description:** بناء تفاعل إيجابي مع الطلاب لزيادة المشاركة والانضباط.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Use Premack principle and behavioral momentum.
- Build motivation through choice and control.
- Prevent power struggles.

**Recommended For:** Teachers, ABA aides, parents.

---

## Course: Managing Challenging Behavior | إدارة السلوكيات الصعبة

**EN Description:** Apply effective behavioral strategies for intervention and prevention of challenging behaviors.

**AR Description:** تطبيق استراتيجيات سلوكية فعالة للوقاية والتدخل.

**Key Outcomes:**
- De-escalate behavioral crises safely.
- Build behavior intervention plans (BIP).
- Apply antecedent and consequence-based strategies.

**Recommended For:** SEN teachers, ABA practitioners, school behavior specialists.

---

## Course: Play Skills & Communication | مهارات اللعب والتواصل

**EN Description:** Use play to enhance communication and social development in children, especially those with autism or developmental delays.

**AR Description:** استخدام اللعب لدعم التواصل والنمو الاجتماعي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Teach functional play skills (solitary, parallel, cooperative).
- Use play as a context for language development.
- Build peer interaction skills.

**Recommended For:** Early intervention specialists, SEN teachers, speech therapists.

---

## Course: Developing Daily Routines | تطوير الروتين اليومي

**EN Description:** Establish consistent routines that support learning, independence, and self-regulation.

**AR Description:** إنشاء روتين ثابت يدعم التعلم والاستقلالية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Build morning, school, and bedtime routines.
- Use visual schedules and task analysis.
- Increase independence through structured routines.

**Recommended For:** Parents, KG teachers, SEN practitioners.

---

## Course: School Readiness | الاستعداد المدرسي

**EN Description:** Prepare children—especially those with developmental needs—for a successful transition to school environments.

**AR Description:** إعداد الأطفال للانتقال السلس إلى البيئة المدرسية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Build pre-academic skills (attention, sitting, following instructions).
- Develop social readiness.
- Manage school transition successfully.

**Recommended For:** Early intervention staff, KG teachers, parents preparing for school entry.

---

## Course: Introduction to Feeding | مقدمة في التغذية

**EN Description:** Apply gradual, safe methods for introducing new foods—particularly for children with feeding aversions or sensory sensitivities.

**AR Description:** تطبيق أساليب آمنة للتدرج في تقديم الأطعمة الجديدة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Recognize feeding disorders vs. picky eating.
- Apply systematic desensitization to food.
- Collaborate with occupational therapists and dietitians.

**Recommended For:** SEN teachers, early intervention specialists, parents of children with feeding issues.

---

## Course: Acceptance & Commitment Training (ACT) for ABA Practitioners | تدريب القبول والالتزام للممارسين

**EN Description:** Promote psychological flexibility and reduce educator/practitioner stress in demanding ABA contexts.

**AR Description:** تعزيز المرونة النفسية وتقليل التوتر لدى الممارسين.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply ACT for practitioner self-care.
- Build resilience in high-demand SEN settings.
- Use mindfulness in clinical practice.

**Recommended For:** ABA practitioners, shadow teachers, SEN staff experiencing burnout.
