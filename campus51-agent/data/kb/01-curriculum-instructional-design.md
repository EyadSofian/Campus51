# Category 01 — Curriculum & Instructional Design | تصميم المناهج وأساليب التعليم

**Audience:** Teachers, HODs, curriculum coordinators, instructional designers.
**Focus:** Designing courses, lessons, assessments, and rubrics that drive measurable learning outcomes.

---

## Course: Instructional Design | تصميم التعليم

**EN Description:** Apply effective design frameworks for planning lessons and courses systematically.

**AR Description:** تطبيق نماذج التصميم الفعّالة لتخطيط الدروس والمقررات بشكل منهجي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Master core instructional design frameworks (ADDIE, SAM, Backwards Design).
- Build clear learning objectives aligned with outcomes.
- Sequence content for maximum retention and engagement.

**Recommended For:** Teachers and HODs designing new units or revising existing curriculum.

---

## Course: Curriculum Mapping for Impact & Alignment | تخطيط المناهج لتحقيق الأثر والمواءمة

**EN Description:** Align objectives, assessments, and teaching methods to achieve the best learning outcomes.

**AR Description:** مواءمة الأهداف والتقييمات وطرق التدريس لتحقيق أفضل نتائج التعلم.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Map standards across grade levels and subjects.
- Identify gaps and overlaps in the current curriculum.
- Design coherent learning pathways across years.

**Recommended For:** Curriculum coordinators, HODs, academic directors, school leadership teams.

---

## Course: Developing Rubrics for Learning & Growth | تطوير معايير التقييم

**EN Description:** Design clear, effective rubrics to measure learner performance, foster continuous growth, and motivate self-development.

**AR Description:** تصميم معايير (Rubrics) واضحة وفعّالة لقياس أداء المتعلمين وتعزيز نموهم المستمر وتحفيز التطوير الذاتي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Build transparent, outcomes-based rubrics.
- Differentiate analytic vs. holistic rubrics.
- Use rubrics to give learners actionable feedback.

**Recommended For:** Teachers across all subjects, assessment leaders.

---

## Course: Designing an Authentic Assessment | تصميم التقييم الأصيل

**EN Description:** Design realistic assessment tasks that reflect deep understanding and the ability to apply knowledge in real-life contexts.

**AR Description:** تصميم أساليب تقييم واقعية تعكس مهارات المتعلمين، بهدف قياس الفهم العميق وقدرتهم على توظيف المعرفة في الحياة العملية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Design real-world tasks that measure applied learning.
- Move beyond multiple-choice to performance-based assessment.
- Develop scoring criteria for open-ended tasks.

**Recommended For:** Teachers seeking to assess higher-order thinking, IB/PYP/MYP educators.

---

## Course: Feedforward Approach in Learning | منهجية التغذية التقدّمية في التعلم

**EN Description:** Provide forward-looking guidance and strategies that help learners improve future performance, rather than only reflecting on past mistakes.

**AR Description:** تقديم توجيهات واستراتيجيات مسبقة تساعد المتعلمين على تحسين أدائهم المستقبلي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Shift feedback from past-focused to future-focused.
- Develop coaching-style guidance.
- Embed feedforward in daily classroom routines.

**Recommended For:** Teachers, mentors, instructional coaches.

---

## Course: Project-Based Learning (PBL) | التعلم القائم على المشاريع

**EN Description:** Organize learning around practical, real-world projects that foster critical thinking, collaboration, creativity, and application of knowledge.

**AR Description:** تنظيم التعلم حول مشاريع عملية وحقيقية، مما يعزز التفكير النقدي والتعاون والإبداع وتطبيق المعرفة في سياقات واقعية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Design driving questions and project arcs.
- Manage student teams and milestones.
- Assess process and final product.

**Recommended For:** Teachers in STEM, humanities, and integrated curriculum settings.

---

## Course: Game-Based Learning | التعلّم القائم على الألعاب

**EN Description:** Use educational games as a tool to enhance learning—boosting motivation, participation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

**AR Description:** استخدام الألعاب التعليمية كأداة لتعزيز التعلم، حيث تُوظّف الألعاب لتحفيز المتعلمين وتعزيز المشاركة وتطوير مهارات التفكير النقدي وحل المشكلات.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Integrate gamification mechanics into lessons.
- Select and design educational games per learning objective.
- Use games for formative assessment.

**Recommended For:** Primary/elementary teachers, EdTech enthusiasts, EFL instructors.

---

## Course: Inquiry-Based Learning | التعلّم القائم على الاستقصاء

**EN Description:** Encourage learners to ask questions, explore concepts, and solve problems independently—fostering critical thinking, scientific curiosity, and learning autonomy.

**AR Description:** تشجيع المتعلمين على طرح الأسئلة، استكشاف المفاهيم، وحل المشكلات بأنفسهم، مما يعزز التفكير النقدي والفضول العلمي والاستقلالية في التعلم.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Design open-ended inquiry questions.
- Scaffold student investigation processes.
- Build a culture of curiosity in the classroom.

**Recommended For:** IB educators, science teachers, primary/elementary teachers.

---

## Course: Assessment & Feedback | التقييم والتغذية الراجعة

**EN Description:** Design effective assessment methods and deliver constructive feedback that helps learners understand their strengths and areas of growth.

**AR Description:** تصميم أساليب تقييم فعّالة وتقديم تغذية راجعة بنّاءة تساعد المتعلمين على فهم نقاط القوة والضعف لديهم.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Balance formative and summative assessment.
- Deliver feedback that drives learning, not just grading.
- Use peer and self-assessment effectively.

**Recommended For:** All teachers, HODs, assessment coordinators.

---

## Course: 5Es Lesson Planning Model | نموذج تخطيط الدروس 5Es

**EN Description:** Use the 5Es model (Engage – Explore – Explain – Elaborate – Evaluate) to design lessons that create systematic, interactive learning experiences and deepen learner understanding.

**AR Description:** استخدام نموذج 5Es لتصميم الدروس، بهدف خلق تجربة تعلمية منهجية، تفاعلية، وتعزز فهم المتعلمين العميق.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Master each phase of the 5Es cycle.
- Build discovery-based lessons.
- Use 5Es for science, math, and integrated subjects.

**Recommended For:** Science teachers, STEAM educators, primary teachers.

---

## Course: Planning Your Unit Backwards | التخطيط العكسي للوحدة الدراسية

**EN Description:** Design instructional units starting from final learning outcomes, then determining assessment methods and activities needed to achieve those outcomes.

**AR Description:** تصميم الوحدة التعليمية بدءًا من تحديد النتائج التعليمية النهائية، ثم تحديد أساليب التقييم والأنشطة اللازمة لتحقيق هذه النتائج.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply Wiggins & McTighe's Understanding by Design framework.
- Start with the end in mind for every unit.
- Align all activities and assessments to outcomes.

**Recommended For:** Curriculum designers, HODs, experienced teachers.

---

## Course: Applying Bloom's Taxonomy in Daily Lessons | تصنيف بلوم في ممارستك اليومية

**EN Description:** Apply the six levels of Bloom's thinking taxonomy to elevate cognitive rigor and student thinking in daily lesson planning and activities.

**AR Description:** تطبيق مستويات التفكير الستة في تصنيف بلوم في التخطيط اليومي للدروس والأنشطة لرفع مستوى التفكير المعرفي للطلاب.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Write learning objectives at every Bloom level.
- Design questions and tasks that target higher-order thinking.
- Audit lesson plans for cognitive balance.

**Recommended For:** All teachers, especially those preparing for QTS/TKT certification.

---

## Course: Competency-Based Learning | التعلّم القائم على الكفاءات

**EN Description:** Develop learner skills and abilities based on pre-defined competencies, with a focus on achieving clear learning outcomes for each learner.

**AR Description:** تطوير مهارات وقدرات المتعلمين بناءً على الكفاءات المحددة مسبقًا، مع التركيز على تحقيق نتائج تعليمية واضحة لكل متعلم.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Define and operationalize learning competencies.
- Design assessment that measures competency, not just content.
- Personalize pathways based on competency progression.

**Recommended For:** Vocational educators, HODs, schools transitioning to outcomes-based models.
