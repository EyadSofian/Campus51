# Category 03 — STEAM and 21st-Century Skills | STEAM ومهارات القرن الحادي والعشرين

**Audience:** STEM/STEAM teachers, math/science educators, integrated curriculum coordinators.
**Focus:** Building integrated learning experiences across Science, Tech, Engineering, Arts, and Math + 21st-century learner skills.

---

## Course: STEM Simulations in Education | المحاكاة في التعليم STEM

**EN Description:** Use interactive simulations across Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) to provide realistic, safe learning environments.

**AR Description:** استخدام المحاكاة التفاعلية في مجالات العلوم، التكنولوجيا، الهندسة، والرياضيات (STEM) لتوفير بيئات تعلمية واقعية وآمنة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Use platforms like PhET, GeoGebra, Tinkercad for instruction.
- Design simulation-based labs.
- Bridge abstract concepts with concrete visual models.

**Recommended For:** Science, physics, chemistry, math teachers.

---

## Course: STEAM Foundation | أساسيات STEAM

**EN Description:** Introduce the foundational concepts of STEAM education, which integrates Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics in integrated learning experiences.

**AR Description:** تقديم المفاهيم الأساسية لتعليم STEAM، الذي يدمج العلوم، التكنولوجيا، الهندسة، الفن، والرياضيات في تجارب تعلمية متكاملة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Understand the philosophy and pedagogy of STEAM.
- Design integrated cross-disciplinary projects.
- Assess interdisciplinary learning outcomes.

**Recommended For:** Primary teachers, integrated curriculum designers, STEAM coordinators.

---

## Course: 21st Century Skills in Education | مهارات القرن الحادي والعشرين في التعليم

**EN Description:** Build critical thinking, collaboration, communication, and creativity (the 4Cs).

**AR Description:** بناء مهارات التفكير النقدي والتعاون والتواصل والإبداع.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Embed 4Cs into existing lessons.
- Design assessments that measure 21st-century skills.
- Prepare learners for a fast-changing workforce.

**Recommended For:** All teachers, especially in international curricula (IB, Cambridge).

---

## Course: Humanizing Math & Metacognition in Mathematics | إضفاء الطابع الإنساني على الرياضيات من خلال التفكير فوق المعرفي

**EN Description:** Cultivate deeper understanding and learner agency by enhancing learners' awareness of their thinking processes, helping them understand concepts more deeply and control their learning strategies.

**AR Description:** تعزيز وعي المتعلمين بأساليب تفكيرهم، مما يساعدهم على فهم المفاهيم بشكل أعمق، التحكم في استراتيجيات التعلم الخاصة بهم.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Transform math anxiety into mathematical confidence.
- Teach metacognitive strategies in math.
- Make math relatable to learners' lives.

**Recommended For:** Math teachers (primary to secondary).

---

## Course: Visible Thinking & Routines | التفكير المرئي والروتينات التعليمية

**EN Description:** Use organized strategies and tools to make learners' thinking visible and clear—embedding thinking routines from frameworks like Project Zero.

**AR Description:** استخدام استراتيجيات وأدوات منظمة لجعل التفكير الفكري للمتعلمين مرئيًا وواضحًا.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply Harvard Project Zero thinking routines.
- Use "See-Think-Wonder", "Claim-Support-Question", etc.
- Build a classroom thinking culture.

**Recommended For:** IB educators, gifted education teachers, all reflective practitioners.

---

## Course: Metacognition in Classroom Practice | التفكير فوق المعرفي في الممارسة الصفية

**EN Description:** Equip learners with strategies to monitor and reflect on their own thinking during learning—including planning, monitoring, and evaluating their learning strategies.

**AR Description:** تمكين المتعلمين من وعي ومراقبة طرق تفكيرهم أثناء التعلم، بما في ذلك التخطيط، المراقبة، وتقييم استراتيجياتهم التعليمية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Teach students to "think about their thinking".
- Build self-regulated learners.
- Use metacognitive scaffolds.

**Recommended For:** All teachers, especially those preparing learners for high-stakes exams.
