# Category 05 — Pedagogy & Learning Strategies | استراتيجيات التعليم والتعلم

**Audience:** All K-12 teachers, especially newly qualified teachers and those refreshing pedagogical practice.
**Focus:** Core teaching strategies, frameworks, and reflective practice.

---

## Course: Approaches to Teaching & Learning | مناهج التعليم والتعلم

**EN Description:** Explore global pedagogical frameworks for effective instruction.

**AR Description:** استكشاف أطر تربوية عالمية للتدريس الفعّال.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Compare constructivist, behaviorist, and cognitivist approaches.
- Match pedagogical approach to learning context.
- Build a personal teaching philosophy.

**Recommended For:** Early-career teachers, those preparing for QTS, iPGCE.

---

## Course: Active Learning in the 21st Century Classroom | التعلم النشط في صفوف القرن الحادي والعشرين

**EN Description:** Shift from teacher-centered to learner-centered approaches by transforming the classroom into an interactive, student-centric environment.

**AR Description:** تحويل بيئة الصف إلى تفاعلية قائمة على المتعلم.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Use Think-Pair-Share, Jigsaw, gallery walks.
- Build engagement through active strategies.
- Move learners from passive recipients to active participants.

**Recommended For:** All teachers transitioning from lecture-based teaching.

---

## Course: Cooperative vs Collaborative Learning | التعلم التعاوني مقابل التشاركي

**EN Description:** Harness group dynamics for deeper engagement and distinguish between cooperative and collaborative learning approaches.

**AR Description:** تمييز الفروق بين التعلّم التعاوني والتشاركي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply Kagan structures for cooperative learning.
- Build collaborative tasks with shared goals.
- Assess individual and group contributions.

**Recommended For:** Teachers using group work, project-based learning practitioners.

---

## Course: Reflective Practice in Education | الممارسة التأملية في التعليم

**EN Description:** Build a culture of continuous self-improvement through systematic reflection on teaching practice.

**AR Description:** بناء ثقافة تطوير ذاتي مستمر.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply Gibbs', Kolb's, and Schön's reflection models.
- Maintain reflective journals.
- Use reflection to drive professional growth.

**Recommended For:** Teachers pursuing QTS/iPGCE, mentors, instructional coaches.

---

## Course: Inquiry & Visible Thinking | التفكير المرئي والاستقصائي

**EN Description:** Promote curiosity and reasoning in daily lessons.

**AR Description:** تعزيز الفضول والتفكير النقدي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Combine inquiry-based learning with visible thinking routines.
- Design lessons around essential questions.
- Make student thinking explicit and assessable.

**Recommended For:** IB educators, secondary teachers, science/humanities staff.

---

## Course: Understanding Activity Design | تصميم الأنشطة التعليمية

**EN Description:** Plan purposeful, outcome-based classroom activities with clear goals and outcomes.

**AR Description:** تخطيط أنشطة صفية ذات هدف ونتائج واضحة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Distinguish activities from busywork.
- Align activities to learning objectives.
- Sequence activities for progressive complexity.

**Recommended For:** All teachers, especially those preparing detailed lesson plans for evaluation.
