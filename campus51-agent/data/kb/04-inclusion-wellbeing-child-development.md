# Category 04 — Inclusion, Wellbeing & Child Development | الشمول والرفاهية وتطور الطفل

**Audience:** SEN teachers, inclusion coordinators, early years educators, school counselors, all classroom teachers.
**Focus:** Building inclusive, emotionally safe classrooms that support every learner.

---

## Course: Differentiation for Inclusion | التفريق في التعليم للدمج الشامل

**EN Description:** Adapt teaching to meet the needs of all learners—creating learning experiences that work for diverse abilities, learning styles, and backgrounds.

**AR Description:** تكييف التعليم لتلبية احتياجات جميع المتعلمين.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Differentiate by content, process, product, and learning environment.
- Use formative assessment to guide differentiation decisions.
- Design tiered tasks and flexible groupings.

**Recommended For:** All classroom teachers, especially in mixed-ability settings.

---

## Course: Diversity & Inclusion | التنوع والدمج

**EN Description:** Foster a fair and inclusive educational environment where every learner feels valued and represented.

**AR Description:** تعزيز بيئة تعليمية عادلة وشاملة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Build culturally responsive teaching practices.
- Address unconscious bias in the classroom.
- Create representation and belonging.

**Recommended For:** International school staff, leaders building school culture.

---

## Course: Understanding Child Development | فهم النمو الطفولي

**EN Description:** Identify developmental milestones and their impact on learning across cognitive, social, emotional, and physical domains.

**AR Description:** التعرف على مراحل النمو وتأثيرها على التعلم.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Map developmental stages (Piaget, Vygotsky, Erikson).
- Identify when behavior is developmental vs concerning.
- Design age-appropriate learning experiences.

**Recommended For:** Early years educators, primary teachers, ABA practitioners.

---

## Course: Safeguarding & Wellbeing / Child Protection | حماية الطفل والرفاه

**EN Description:** Develop preventive practices and support mental health, including recognizing signs of distress, abuse, or neglect, and following safeguarding procedures.

**AR Description:** تطوير الممارسات الوقائية ودعم الصحة النفسية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Recognize warning signs of abuse or distress.
- Apply school safeguarding policies.
- Build trauma-informed practice.

**Recommended For:** All school staff (mandatory in many international curricula).

---

## Course: Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) | التعلم الاجتماعي العاطفي

**EN Description:** Support emotional literacy, empathy, and self-management—developing students' skills in recognizing emotions, managing them, and interacting positively with others.

**AR Description:** تطوير مهارات الطلاب في التعرف على العواطف وإدارتها، والتفاعل الإيجابي مع الآخرين.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Teach the 5 CASEL competencies (self-awareness, self-management, social awareness, relationship skills, decision-making).
- Embed SEL into daily lessons.
- Use SEL assessment frameworks.

**Recommended For:** All teachers, school counselors, homeroom teachers.

---

## Course: Positive Discipline in Action | الانضباط الإيجابي في الممارسة

**EN Description:** Manage behavior through understanding and structure rather than punishment.

**AR Description:** إدارة السلوك من خلال التفهم والهيكلة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply Jane Nelsen's Positive Discipline principles.
- Replace punitive measures with restorative practices.
- Build a respectful classroom culture.

**Recommended For:** All teachers, especially those facing behavioral challenges.

---

## Course: Effective Routines for Early Learners | الروتينات الفعالة للمتعلمين الصغار

**EN Description:** Build consistent habits and routines that support learner independence and structure in early years and primary classrooms.

**AR Description:** بناء عادات وروتينات تدعم الاستقلالية والنظام.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Design morning routines, transitions, and end-of-day rituals.
- Use visual schedules and predictability for young learners.
- Build self-regulation through routine.

**Recommended For:** Early years teachers, KG-Grade 2 educators.

---

## Course: Acceptance & Commitment Therapy / Training (ACT) | تدريب القبول والالتزام

**EN Description:** Promote psychological flexibility, focus, internal motivation, and reduce educator stress for personal and professional growth.

**AR Description:** تعزيز المرونة النفسية، التركيز، التحفيز الداخلي لتحقيق النمو الشخصي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Build psychological flexibility for teachers.
- Apply ACT principles to teacher wellbeing.
- Use mindfulness and values-based action.

**Recommended For:** Teachers experiencing burnout, school wellbeing coordinators.
