# Category 06 — English Language Teaching (ELT) and Literacy | تدريس اللغة الإنجليزية والقراءة

**Audience:** English teachers (ESL/EFL), literacy coaches, those pursuing Cambridge ELT certifications.
**Focus:** Teaching English as a second/foreign language, literacy development, internationally-recognized ELT qualifications.

---

## Course: Teaching English through Drama | تدريس اللغة الإنجليزية من خلال الدراما

**EN Description:** Use performance-based strategies (role-play, dramatization, theatrical scenarios) to enhance fluency and confidence in English learners.

**AR Description:** استخدام التمثيل لتعزيز الطلاقة والثقة لدى الطلاب.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Use drama techniques to break language barriers.
- Build student confidence in speaking and presentation.
- Integrate movement, voice, and emotion into language learning.

**Recommended For:** EFL/ESL teachers, primary and secondary English teachers.

---

## Course: Guided Reading in Teaching Language | القراءة الموجهة في تعليم اللغة

**EN Description:** Develop vocabulary and comprehension through structured, guided reading sessions.

**AR Description:** تطوير المفردات والفهم من خلال القراءة الموجهة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Lead small-group guided reading sessions.
- Select texts at appropriate reading levels.
- Use guided reading for differentiation.

**Recommended For:** Primary ELT teachers, literacy coaches.

---

## Course: Balancing the Four Skills | موازنة المهارات الأربع

**EN Description:** Effectively integrate reading, writing, listening, and speaking in language instruction.

**AR Description:** دمج القراءة والكتابة والاستماع والمحادثة بفعالية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Design lessons that cycle through all 4 skills.
- Avoid over-reliance on any single skill.
- Build integrated assessment tasks.

**Recommended For:** All ELT teachers, especially those preparing for TKT.

---

## Course: Cultivating Your Classroom Culture | بناء ثقافة صفية إيجابية

**EN Description:** Create engaging, learner-centered English classrooms with a positive culture for continuous growth.

**AR Description:** بناء ثقافة تطوير ذاتي مستمر.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Build a low-anxiety, high-input classroom environment.
- Use rituals and routines to reinforce English use.
- Foster student ownership of language learning.

**Recommended For:** EFL teachers, especially in mixed-proficiency classrooms.

---

## Course: Preparing for TKT (Teaching Knowledge Test) | التحضير لاختبار TKT

**EN Description:** TKT (Teaching Knowledge Test) is an internationally recognized Cambridge English qualification designed specifically for English language teachers, granting internationally accredited credentials in teaching skills and opening new professional horizons.

**AR Description:** اختبار TKT هو امتحان تابع لجامعة كامبريدج صُمم خصيصًا لمعلمي اللغة الإنجليزية. تمنح هذه المؤهلات الدولية المعترف بها تقييمًا لمهارات التدريس وفتح آفاق مهنية جديدة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Master TKT Modules 1, 2, and 3 content.
- Practice past papers and sample answers.
- Build confidence for the international exam.

**Recommended For:** EFL teachers seeking international Cambridge certification.

---

## Course: DELTA — Diploma in Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages | دبلوم تدريس اللغة الإنجليزية للناطقين بغيرها

**EN Description:** DELTA is an advanced, internationally recognized diploma for experienced English language teachers, focused on developing advanced skills in ELT, curriculum design, and student assessment.

**AR Description:** يُعد هذا الدبلوم برنامجًا متقدمًا للمعلمين ذوي الخبرة، يركز على تطوير المهارات المتقدمة في تدريس اللغة الإنجليزية، تصميم المناهج التعليمية، وتقييم الطلاب.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Deepen ELT methodology expertise.
- Design and evaluate ELT curricula.
- Lead in international ELT institutions.

**Recommended For:** Experienced ELT teachers (3+ years), HODs, English language coordinators.

**Prerequisites:** Bachelor's degree + minimum 2 years teaching experience.
