# Category 07 — Educational Leadership and Professional Growth | القيادة التعليمية والنمو المهني

**Audience:** Principals, vice-principals, HODs, middle leaders, aspiring leaders, school owners.
**Focus:** Leadership skills, instructional leadership, coaching, professional values, and growth mindset for educators.

---

## Course: Educational Leadership | القيادة التعليمية

**EN Description:** Develop leadership skills for teachers and administrators in schools—including team management, strategic decision-making, and inspiring the educational environment.

**AR Description:** تطوير المهارات القيادية للمعلمين والإداريين في المدارس، بما يشمل إدارة الفرق، اتخاذ القرارات الاستراتيجية، وتحفيز البيئة التعليمية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Build vision, strategy, and team empowerment.
- Lead change in schools.
- Communicate effectively with stakeholders (parents, board, staff).

**Recommended For:** Principals, vice-principals, aspiring school leaders.

---

## Course: Six Levers for Elevating Middle Leadership | الركائز الست لرفع مستوى القيادة الوسطى

**EN Description:** Strengthen instructional leadership for middle managers with practical strategies and tools to enhance effectiveness, including team management, leadership development, and balancing educational and organizational goals.

**AR Description:** تقديم استراتيجيات وأدوات عملية لتعزيز فعالية القادة الوسطى في المدارس، بما يشمل إدارة الفرق، تطوير المهارات القيادية، وتحقيق التوازن بين الأهداف التعليمية والتنظيمية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Master the 6 levers of effective middle leadership.
- Bridge between senior leadership and teaching teams.
- Lead instructional improvement at department level.

**Recommended For:** HODs, year leaders, subject coordinators, deputy heads.

---

## Course: Coaching & Mentoring in Education | التوجيه والإرشاد في التعليم

**EN Description:** Develop a coaching culture within schools through professional and educational guidance and support of staff.

**AR Description:** بناء ثقافة الإرشاد داخل المدارس.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply the GROW coaching model.
- Differentiate coaching, mentoring, and supervision.
- Build a school-wide coaching culture.

**Recommended For:** Middle and senior leaders, instructional coaches, experienced teachers becoming mentors.

---

## Course: Teachers as Researchers | المعلمون كباحثين

**EN Description:** Promote evidence-based educational research and practitioner inquiry for professional growth.

**AR Description:** تعزيز البحث التربوي والممارسات المستندة إلى الأدلة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Design action research projects in your classroom.
- Use data to drive instructional decisions.
- Contribute to the wider profession through reflective inquiry.

**Recommended For:** Reflective practitioners, MA students, those pursuing iPGCE.

---

## Course: Professionalism in the Work Environment | الاحترافية في بيئة العمل

**EN Description:** Promote professional values and ethical conduct in the educational workplace.

**AR Description:** تعزيز القيم المهنية والسلوك الأخلاقي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Build professional communication standards.
- Navigate workplace ethics.
- Develop a professional identity.

**Recommended For:** New teachers, support staff, all educators.

---

## Course: Growth Mindset in Education | عقلية النمو في التعليم

**EN Description:** Develop learners' and educators' understanding that abilities and skills can grow through persistence and continuous practice.

**AR Description:** تطوير فهم المتعلمين والمعلمين لفكرة أن القدرات والمهارات يمكن تنميتها بالمثابرة والممارسة المستمرة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply Carol Dweck's mindset research.
- Build classroom language that promotes growth.
- Shift from fixed-mindset to growth-mindset culture.

**Recommended For:** All teachers, school leaders building learning culture.

---

## Course: Emotional Intelligence in the Classroom | الذكاء العاطفي في الصف

**EN Description:** Develop teachers' ability to recognize their own emotions and students' emotions, and manage them effectively.

**AR Description:** تطوير قدرة المعلمين على التعرف على عواطفهم وعواطف الطلاب وإدارتها بفاعلية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Build self-awareness and emotional regulation.
- Read and respond to student emotional cues.
- Model emotional intelligence for learners.

**Recommended For:** All teachers, especially homeroom and pastoral leads.
