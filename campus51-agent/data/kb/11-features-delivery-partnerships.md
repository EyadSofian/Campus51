# Campus 51 — Features, Delivery & Partnerships | المميزات وطرق التقديم والشراكات

## Delivery Modes | طرق التقديم

Campus 51 programs are delivered in three flexible modes:

### 1. Live | مباشر
Real-time virtual or in-person sessions with instructors. Best for interactive discussions, immediate feedback, and cohort-based learning.

### 2. Video | فيديو
Self-paced video courses. Best for busy professionals who need flexibility around teaching schedules.

### 3. Blended | مدمج
Combination of live sessions, video content, mentoring, and assessment. The most popular mode for QTS and certification pathways.

## Certification | الاعتمادات

### CPD-Accredited (UK)
Continuing Professional Development credits recognized internationally — particularly valuable for educators in British curriculum schools and those seeking international roles.

### Cambridge / Campus 51 Endorsement
Globally trusted endorsement of program quality, recognized in international school recruitment.

### TKT Preparation
Direct preparation for the **Cambridge Teaching Knowledge Test** — an internationally recognized credential for English language teachers.

### DELTA Pathway
Advanced **Diploma in Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages** for experienced ELT educators.

### QTS Certification (via Assessment Route)
Successful candidates receive official **Qualified Teacher Status** and a **Teacher Reference Number (TRN)** registered through the UK Department for Education.

### ABA Certifications (RBT / IBT / QBT)
Preparation pathways for international Behavior Technician certifications.

## Partnership Tracks | مسارات الشراكة

Campus 51 offers institutional partnerships through three models:

### 1. White-Labeled Courses
Courses delivered under your school or institution's brand, powered by Campus 51's curriculum and instructors.

### 2. Co-Certified Programs
Joint certification between Campus 51 and the partner institution — combining brand credibility with international accreditation.

### 3. Revenue-Shared Accreditations
Partner institutions distribute Campus 51 programs and share revenue while leveraging international CPD/Cambridge accreditation.
