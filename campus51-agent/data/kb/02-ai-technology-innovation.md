# Category 02 — AI, Technology and Innovation in Education | الذكاء الاصطناعي والتكنولوجيا والابتكار في التعليم

**Audience:** Teachers, HODs, and school leaders adopting AI and EdTech tools.
**Focus:** Integrating AI tools, digital platforms, and innovative tech responsibly in K-12 classrooms.

---

## Course: AI Revolution in Education | ثورة الذكاء الاصطناعي في التعليم

**EN Description:** Understand how AI is reshaping teaching and learning across subjects, and explore the impact of AI on assessment methods and pedagogical approaches.

**AR Description:** فهم كيفية تأثير الذكاء الاصطناعي على أساليب التدريس والتعلم وتأثيره على طرق التقييم والمنهجيات التعليمية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Map AI's impact across instructional roles.
- Identify ethical AI integration opportunities.
- Prepare schools for the AI transition.

**Recommended For:** Principals, EdTech leads, forward-thinking teachers.

---

## Course: Rethinking Assessment in the Age of AI | إعادة التفكير في التقييم في عصر الذكاء الاصطناعي

**EN Description:** Explore innovative assessment methods suitable for AI integration, including digital tools for deep, personalized, and interactive assessment.

**AR Description:** استكشاف طرق تقييم مبتكرة تتناسب مع استخدام الذكاء الاصطناعي في التعليم، بما يشمل توظيف الأدوات الرقمية لتعزيز التقييم العميق، الشخصي، والتفاعلي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Design AI-resistant authentic assessments.
- Use AI tools to enhance (not replace) feedback.
- Maintain academic integrity in AI-enabled environments.

**Recommended For:** Assessment coordinators, HODs, secondary teachers.

---

## Course: AI Tools in Classroom Practice | أدوات الذكاء الاصطناعي في الممارسات الصفية

**EN Description:** Apply practical AI tools for lesson design, formative assessment, and instructional support in real classroom settings.

**AR Description:** تطبيق أدوات عملية للذكاء الاصطناعي في تصميم الدروس والتقييم التكويني.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Use ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and similar tools for lesson planning.
- Build AI-powered formative assessments.
- Integrate AI into differentiation strategies.

**Recommended For:** Tech-savvy teachers, EdTech coordinators.

---

## Course: Ethical Considerations in the AI Era | الاعتبارات الأخلاقية في عصر الذكاء الاصطناعي

**EN Description:** Address issues of privacy, bias, data ethics, and responsible AI use in educational contexts.

**AR Description:** معالجة قضايا الخصوصية والتحيز والبيانات الأخلاقية في الاستخدام التعليمي للذكاء الاصطناعي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Recognize algorithmic bias in EdTech.
- Build school-wide AI ethics policies.
- Teach students AI literacy and critical evaluation.

**Recommended For:** Principals, IT leads, all educators.

---

## Course: Digital Technology in Teaching and Learning | التكنولوجيا الرقمية في التعليم والتعلم

**EN Description:** Integrate digital platforms to increase student engagement and enhance the teaching-learning experience.

**AR Description:** دمج المنصات الرقمية لزيادة تفاعل الطلاب.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Evaluate and select EdTech platforms.
- Design tech-enhanced learning experiences.
- Avoid "tech for tech's sake" pitfalls.

**Recommended For:** Teachers transitioning from analog to digital classrooms.

## Course: Adaptive Learning | التعلم التكيفي

**EN Description:** Use intelligent systems to personalize learning according to each student's needs, pace, and progress.

**AR Description:** استخدام الأنظمة الذكية لتخصيص التعلم وفق احتياجات الطالب.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Implement adaptive learning platforms.
- Design pathways for differentiated learners.
- Use data to drive personalization decisions.

**Recommended For:** Schools investing in personalized learning, SEN coordinators.

---

## Course: Digital Storytelling | السرد الرقمي

**EN Description:** Use narrative technology to inspire creativity and critical thinking in learners.

**AR Description:** استخدام التكنولوجيا القصصية لإلهام الإبداع والتفكير النقدي.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Design story-driven digital lessons.
- Use multimedia tools (video, audio, animation) effectively.
- Foster student voice through digital narratives.

**Recommended For:** English/Arabic language teachers, primary teachers, creative arts educators.

---

## Course: Utilizing Digital Tools for Classroom Management | استخدام الأدوات الرقمية لإدارة الصف

**EN Description:** Optimize classroom workflows and improve in-class operations using digital education tools.

**AR Description:** تحسين سير العمل في الصف باستخدام الأدوات التعليمية الرقمية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Use tools like ClassDojo, Google Classroom, Microsoft Teams effectively.
- Streamline attendance, grading, and parent communication.
- Reduce admin time and reclaim instructional time.

**Recommended For:** Classroom teachers at all levels.

---

## Course: Organizing Blended Learning Models in Practice | تنظيم نماذج التعلم المدمج

**EN Description:** Design hybrid classrooms that combine synchronous (in-person) and asynchronous (online) learning effectively.

**AR Description:** تصميم بيئات تعلم تجمع بين التعلم المباشر والإلكتروني.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply rotational, flex, and à-la-carte blended models.
- Sequence in-person vs online activities for max impact.
- Manage hybrid student engagement.

**Recommended For:** Schools post-COVID transitioning to permanent blended models.

---

## Course: Designing Lessons in a Virtual Environment | تصميم الدروس في بيئة التعلم الافتراضي

**EN Description:** Plan and deliver interactive online sessions using innovative methods that keep learners engaged virtually.

**AR Description:** إعداد جلسات تفاعلية عبر الإنترنت بطرق مبتكرة.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Design for synchronous online engagement.
- Use breakout rooms, polls, and collaborative tools.
- Manage virtual classroom behavior and pacing.

**Recommended For:** Online teachers, hybrid school staff.

---

## Course: Designing an Inclusive Digital Lesson | تصميم دروس رقمية شاملة

**EN Description:** Ensure inclusivity and easy access in digital learning environments, particularly for learners with special needs or limited access.

**AR Description:** ضمان الشمولية وسهولة الوصول في بيئات التعلم الرقمية.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Apply UDL (Universal Design for Learning) principles to digital lessons.
- Make digital content accessible (alt text, captions, screen-reader friendly).
- Support diverse learners in virtual settings.

**Recommended For:** SEN teachers, inclusion coordinators, digital lesson designers.
